Cox's Bazar Package

Tour To Cox's Bazar - 4 Nights / 3 Days

4 Nights / 3 Days | Inani Beach, Himchhari Falls. Back to coxs bazar sea beach. Enjoy Sunset and night beach, Overnight at Cox's Bazar.

Starting From

Tk 9000

(Price Per Person)

:: Tour Plan ::

Dhaka - Cox's Bazar - Dhaka

Explore Cox's Bazar

Tour Duration : 4 Nights 3 Days (2 Nights stay at Cox's Bazar)

Day 01:

Leave Dhaka for Cox's Bazar by reserved car/coach - Overnight journey.

Day 02:

Cox's Bazar at 08 am. Breakfast and short beach tour. After lunch, start journey to Inani Beach, Himchhari Falls. Back to coxs bazar sea beach. Enjoy Sunset and night beach, Overnight at Cox's Bazar.

Day 03:

Enjoy the morning beach. After lunch, start for Moheshkhali Island by boat. Back to cox's bazar and visit barmiz market. Overnight at Cox's Bazar.

Day 04:

After Breakfast, pack-up baggages and start for Dulahazara Safari Park. back to cox's bazar in the evening. Take dinner. Start for Dhaka by Night Coach. Overnight Journey.

Day 05:

Reach Dhaka at Morning.

- End of Tour -

Note: We need minimum 4 pax to execute this tour program.

TOUR PACKAGE SERVICE INCLUDES:

  1. 1) All Standard / Deluxe accommodation on Twin Sharing basis as per itinerary
  2. AC / Non ac Transport / Ship / Local Transport / Boat / Trollar
  3. All sightseeing, toll, tax and entry fees as per mentioned itinerary
  4. Experienced Guide

TOUR PACKAGE SERVICE EXCLUDES:

  1. Food & Soft Drinks (let us know if you wish to include this)
  2. Items of personal nature and items not mentioned above.
